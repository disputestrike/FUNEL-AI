# GoFunnelAI KB Pack — Business Coaching (US-EN)

> Pack ID: `coaching-business-us-en` | Vertical: Business / executive / sales / agency coaches (B2B, $3k–$60k programs) | Geo: US | Language: EN

## 1. Market Overview

US business coaching market is $14.2B in 2025, growing 6–8% YoY [ICF 2024 Global Coaching Study + IBISWorld]. Approximately 36,500 active business coaches in the US (ICF). Average program price: $2,400 group coaching, $9,800 small-group cohort, $24,000 1:1 6-month, $48,000–$96,000 mastermind/year-long. LTV: 1.6x program price (renewal + upsell). Acceptable CAC: 18–28% of program price. Acceptable CPL (application/booked call): $48–$280 depending on offer price.

Sales cycle: 5–22 days from first opt-in to enrollment for <$10k offers; 14–60 days for $20k+ offers. Application funnel + sales call is the dominant model; webinar-to-enroll is fading post-2023. Group/cohort programs (8–25 students) are highest-margin.

Buying triggers: (1) revenue plateau ($30k/mo, $100k/mo, $1M/yr ceilings); (2) new VP-Sales hire deciding on training; (3) burnout / "I'm the bottleneck"; (4) team scaling stress; (5) economic uncertainty (recession-driven up-skilling); (6) seasonal Q1 planning; (7) new fiscal year budget; (8) podcast/event attendance.

## 2. Buyer Personas

**Persona 1: Solopreneur Stuck at $30k/mo (28%).** Service business owner, 32–48. JTBD: "Break $1M/yr without working 70-hr weeks." Verbatim: "I'm the business." Winning: systems-and-team coach with case studies in their niche.

**Persona 2: Agency Owner $80k–$300k/mo MRR (18%).** Marketing/dev/consulting agency, 30–45. JTBD: "Productize + hire ops leader." Winning: agency-specific operator (not generic biz coach).

**Persona 3: Sales Leader / VP Sales (16%).** 35–55, B2B SaaS or service. JTBD: "Lift team quota attainment." Winning: process-rigorous (not motivational). Budget owned, not personal.

**Persona 4: New Course Creator (14%).** Side-business to full-time. JTBD: "Make the offer convert." Winning: launch + funnel specialist.

**Persona 5: Burned-Out 7-Figure Founder (12%).** $1M–$10M biz, exhausted. JTBD: "Get my life back without losing revenue." Winning: ops + EOS/Traction-style operator.

**Persona 6: Corporate Exec (12%).** Director–VP, $200k+ comp. JTBD: "Promotion or pivot to consulting." Winning: executive presence + LinkedIn-credible coach.

## 11. Prohibited Claims

## 12. Compliance Rules

**FTC Section 5 deceptive earnings claims.** **FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255):** material connections must be disclosed, typical-results disclosure for atypical testimonials. **FTC Business Opportunity Rule (16 CFR Part 437)** if structured as biz-op. **TCPA / CAN-SPAM.** **State UDAP** acts. **TILA / Reg Z** if offering financing or "buy-now-pay-later" terms. **Refund policy disclosure** — material terms before charge. **ROSCA** for negative-option / auto-renew. **ADA/WCAG 2.1 AA.** Mandatory: typical-results / "your results may vary" disclosures on all testimonials.
