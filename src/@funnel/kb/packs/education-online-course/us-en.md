# GoFunnelAI KB Pack — Online Courses / Info Products (US-EN)

> Pack ID: `education-online-course-us-en` | Vertical: Self-paced + cohort-based online courses, info-product creators, Teachable/Kajabi/Thinkific operators | Geo: US | Language: EN

## 1. Market Overview

US online course / info-product market is $42B in 2025, growing 13–18% YoY [Global Industry Analysts]. Approximately 1.4M creators with paid digital products in US. Average prices: low-ticket $27–$197, mid-ticket $497–$1,997, high-ticket cohort $2,500–$9,997. AOV blended ~$340. LTV: 1.4–2.2x first-order if upsells + memberships. Acceptable CAC: 32–58% of AOV. Acceptable CPL: $4–$48 depending on tier.

Sales cycle: minutes to 14 days. Webinar-to-sale ("perfect webinar" funnels) still dominant for $497–$1,997 offers, though declining vs short-form video → tripwire models. Open-cart / closed-cart launches drive 40%+ of annual revenue for cohort creators.

Buying triggers: (1) career anxiety / layoff; (2) side-hustle interest; (3) skill gap at work; (4) New Year goals; (5) influencer endorsement; (6) BFCM discount; (7) FOMO of cohort closing; (8) niche-event launch.

**Persona 2: Aspiring Side-Hustler (24%).** 26–40. JTBD: "Build income stream alongside job." Winning: realistic income examples (with disclosure), step-by-step framework.

**Persona 3: Career Switcher (16%).** 30–48. JTBD: "Pivot careers without grad school." Winning: full bootcamp-style with portfolio.

**Persona 4: Hobby-to-Pro Creator (12%).** 25–55. JTBD: "Turn hobby into business." Winning: business + creative blend.

**Persona 5: Information Junkie (10%).** Buys lots of courses, finishes few. JTBD: "Latest framework." Winning: cohort accountability.

**Persona 6: Employer-Reimbursed Pro (6%).** Manager-approved learning budget. JTBD: "Spend annual L&D allowance." Winning: invoice, certificate, completion tracking.

## 11. Prohibited Claims

Per FTC Section 5, FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR 255), FTC weight-loss/business-opportunity actions, ROSCA, Click-to-Cancel Rule, state UDAP: no income guarantees, no "make $10k/mo guaranteed," no fake scarcity, no fake countdowns, no fake testimonials, no influencer endorsements without #ad, no auto-renew without ROSCA pre-purchase disclosure, no misleading refund claims, no inflated "was $X now $Y" without genuine prior sales at the higher price.

**Hard-block exact strings:**
- `"guaranteed income"`
- `"make $10k a month guaranteed"`
- `"earn 6 figures guaranteed"`
- `"passive income guaranteed"`
- `"financial freedom guaranteed"`
- `"#1 course online"`
- `"the only course you need"`
- `"100% finish rate"`
- `"100% success rate"`
- `"never been done before"`
- `"secret framework"`
- `"act before midnight"`
- `"only 5 spots ever"`
- `"normally $5,000 — today only $497"` (without history)
- `"closing tonight forever"` (unless true)
- `"free trial then auto-charge"` (without ROSCA)
- `"copy my exact $1M system"`
- `"no experience required earn money"`
- `"FTC approved"` (no such thing)
- `"endorsed by Forbes"` (without disclosure)

## 12. Compliance Rules

**FTC Section 5 deceptive earnings claims.** **FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR 255):** #ad, typical-results, material connection. **FTC Business Opportunity Rule (16 CFR 437)** if structured as biz-op. **ROSCA (15 USC 8403) + Click-to-Cancel Rule** for subscriptions. **TCPA + 10DLC.** **CAN-SPAM.** **State UDAP** acts (esp. CA, NY, MA). **TILA / Reg Z** for 4-pay+ financing. **GDPR/CCPA/CPRA** for data. **ADA / WCAG 2.1 AA.** **Influencer #ad** disclosure mandatory. **Refund policy** disclosed before charge. Typical-results / "results not typical" disclaimer on testimonials.

## 13. Form Fields
