## 11. Prohibited Claims

Banned per FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR §255) and FTC Income Claims guidance: typical-results disclosure required for testimonials; cannot promise specific income or transformation timelines without substantiation; "guaranteed [X result]" is high-risk; "make $10k/month in 30 days" is a hard block category; cherry-picked testimonials must disclose typical results; influencer #ad required.

**Hard-block exact strings:**
- `"guaranteed $10k/month"`

- `"guaranteed 7-figure"`
- `"make $X in [time]"` (without typical-results)
- `"100% guaranteed transformation"`

## 12. Compliance Rules

**FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR §255):** testimonials require material connection disclosure; typical results disclosure (post-2023 update — generic disclaimer alone insufficient); influencer #ad required. **FTC Act §5:** substantiate income / outcome claims. **FTC ROSCA (15 USC §8401):** clear disclosure of recurring billing; easy cancellation; affirmative consent. **FTC "Negative Option" Rule (2024):** click-to-cancel parity. **State unauthorized practice of medicine / counseling laws:** coaches cannot diagnose/treat mental health — must refer to licensed providers. **State licensing for therapy/counseling** strictly enforced; coaching is unregulated but title-protection laws apply ("therapist" / "counselor" / "psychologist" restricted in most states). **TCPA / CAN-SPAM.** **State Consumer Protection statutes.** **California AB-587 + similar transparency laws.** **GDPR / CCPA / CPRA** if any EU/CA data. **CAN-SPAM** unsubscribe processing. **ADA / WCAG 2.1 AA.**
