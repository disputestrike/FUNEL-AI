## SECTION 1 — Event Format Overview

### 1.1 The shape of the show
- **Duration:** 30 hours continuous, no scheduled dark periods. Founder hosts ~70% of segments; rotating co-hosts cover sleep windows and founder breathers.
- **Structure:** ~1 hour per industry segment (50 minutes live content + 10 minute crossfade with a pre-recorded package).
- **Cadence:** Opening keynote (Hour 0, 15 min) â†’ 25 industry hours â†’ 3 international/sleep relays â†’ 1 closing keynote (Hour 29, 10 min). Three full breaks at Hour 8, 16, 24 (founder swap-out; co-host bridges 20 min while founder eats/rests).
- **Theme through-line:** every hour we generate a real funnel for a real business in a real industry, live, on camera, end-to-end. No simulators. No mockups. Real outputs, real publishes, real leads where legally possible.

### 1.2 Simulcast plan
- **YouTube Live** — primary canonical stream. 1080p60, dual-camera (founder + screen capture), all chapter markers tagged for VOD.
- **LinkedIn Live** — primary for B2B audience. Same video feed; comments triaged by a dedicated mod.
- **X Live (video)** — embedded on the @funnel handle, repost from founder personal handle every two hours.
- **TikTok Live** — vertical reframe, single-cam founder + lower-third demo PiP. Shorter highlight clips auto-spliced every 15 min and dropped as native TikToks.
- **X Spaces (audio-only)** — runs continuously, hosted by community team, separate guest list, conversations spin off threads to drive secondary social.

### 1.3 On-screen live counters (always visible, lower-third)
- Funnels generated during stream
- Leads captured during stream (consented, anonymized)
- Ads launched (Meta + Google count, by hour)
- Estimated revenue created (sum of leads Ã— avg lead value by industry from KB Packs)
- Concurrent viewers (across all platforms, deduplicated within 5-min tumbling window)

### 1.4 Hourly milestone unlocks
- Counters drive perk unlocks (see Section 5). Each unlock is announced live and pushed to email + SMS to the waitlist + posted across all socials within 60 seconds. A standing pre-built creative pack lives in `/launch-assets/milestone-cards/` so the social team only needs to drop the number in.

### 1.5 Pre-recorded packages (the "B-side")
- 30 packages, 4–9 minutes each, slotted between live segments to give the founder rest and recover the team.
- Mix: 12 customer testimonials, 6 founder explainers (recorded in week 11), 6 "how it's built" engineering deep-dives, 6 industry-expert interviews recorded in advance.
- Every package ends with a CTA card that times out to the next live segment's countdown.

### 1.6 Control room
- Producer + technical director + 2 chat mods + 1 graphics op + 1 social op = 6-person live control crew, on shift in 6-hour blocks (3 shifts Ã— 2 crews = 12 humans total over the 30 hours, plus founder).
- Two redundant uplinks (primary fiber, LTE bonded failover). Restream as switcher of record. Three-camera switch (founder wide, founder tight, screen). OBS as backup capture if Restream croaks.
