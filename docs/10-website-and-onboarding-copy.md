> **Voice rules** (apply everywhere):
> 1. Speak to one person, not a market. "You," not "businesses."
> 2. Verbs over nouns. "Launch," not "deployment."
> 3. Specific over abstract. "Type 8 words," not "easy setup."
> 4. Earn the wit. One wink per screen, max.
> 5. Never call yourself "revolutionary," "next-gen," or "AI-powered." Show, don't claim.
> 6. Numbers beat adjectives. "60 seconds" > "fast."
> 7. Promise what we deliver. We say "a customer," not "a lead" — because RevTry closes the loop.

GDPR cookie banner. CCPA disclosure. CAN-SPAM unsubscribe path. Industry-specific (HIPAA, FCRA, FTC endorsement guidelines). We flag what would trip a regulator.

**Solar**
Homeowners researching solar are stuck between fear of the upfront cost and the math that says it pays back in 7 years. Funnel leads with the bill comparison (your bill today vs. your bill with solar), uses utility-rate-aware copy, and qualifies for roof type, ownership, and credit before RevTry books the site survey.

**Roofing**
Roofing leads are emergency-driven (storm) or maintenance-driven (age + insurance). Funnel reads the local weather feed and rewrites your hook within 4 hours of a hailstorm in your zip code. RevTry asks about insurance claim status and books the inspector.

**HVAC**
Replacement vs. repair decision is the whole sale. Funnel builds two paths — one for the AC-died-today emergency, one for the fall maintenance plan — and RevTry triages by urgency, age of unit, and whether they own the home.

**Pest Control**
Most pest leads convert in under 10 minutes or never. Funnel hooks on the specific pest (termites vs. roaches vs. mosquitos), shows local infestation data, and RevTry books the same-day inspection while the lead is still on the page.

**Real Estate**
For listing agents, Funnel builds a home-value-CMA-magnet that pulls Zestimate-adjacent data and routes to RevTry, who books the listing appointment. For buyer's agents, it builds a neighborhood-tour funnel with a 14-day nurture sequence.

**Mortgage**
Rate-driven traffic with brutal compliance requirements. Funnel handles the APR disclosure, NMLS ID display, and equal housing logo automatically. RevTry asks the four pre-qual questions (credit range, down payment, income, target home price) before booking.

**Insurance**
P&C insurance: Funnel quotes-shop with a multi-carrier hook. Life insurance: Funnel builds a "term vs. whole" educational funnel. Health: Funnel routes by state and qualifies for ACA vs. private vs. Medicare.

**Auto Dealerships**
Lead-on-the-lot is dead. New leads come from inventory pages. Funnel pulls your live inventory feed (via dealer.com or homenet), builds VIN-specific landing pages, and RevTry confirms the lead is real before sending to the BDC.

**Dental**
Cosmetic dentistry (high-ticket, image-driven) and family dentistry (insurance-driven) need different funnels. Funnel builds either, handles HIPAA, and RevTry verifies insurance before booking the consult.

**Med Spa**
Botox, fillers, body contouring, hair removal — each is a different funnel. Funnel uses before/after compliance copy, qualifies by treatment area, and books the consult with the right injector.

**Chiropractic**
