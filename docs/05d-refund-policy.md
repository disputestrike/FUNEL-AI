## 2. 14-Day First-Paid-Month Refund

2.1 If you are dissatisfied with your **first paid month** on any GoFunnelAI paid plan, you may request a **full refund** within **14 calendar days** of the date that first paid charge was processed.

2.2 The 14-day window applies only to the **first paid month per account** and does not reset on subsequent renewals, plan changes, or new subscriptions tied to the same account, payment method, or identity.

2.3 The refund does not apply to:

- annual plans (covered separately in Section 3);
- usage-based fees already consumed (Section 4);
- add-ons separately purchased;
- accounts terminated for breach of the Terms of Service or AUP.

2.4 To request, email `billing@gofunnelai.com` from your Account email or use in-product "Help â†’ Request refund."

## 4. Usage-Based Fees — No Refunds

4.1 The following are **non-refundable once consumed**:

- **RevTry voice minutes** — once a call is placed or received and minutes accrue;
- **Premium AI model overages** — tokens, image generations, audio generations, or video frames generated beyond your plan allowance;
- **Outbound messaging volume** — emails or SMS that have been queued for delivery, dispatched to a gateway, or delivered;
- **Pay-as-you-go credits** — once consumed; unused credits expire per their stated terms;
- **Third-party pass-through costs** — telephony carrier fees, SMS aggregator fees, payment-processor fees, ad-platform spend.

4.2 Where a failure on our side prevented delivery (e.g., a Service outage that caused message-send failures), we will provide a service credit for the affected units, applied to your next invoice.

## 5. Chargeback Policy

5.1 We prefer to resolve disputes directly. Please email `billing@gofunnelai.com` before initiating a chargeback so we can investigate.

5.2 If you initiate a **chargeback we reasonably believe is improper or fraudulent**, we may:

- **suspend your Account immediately** pending resolution;
- **collect** the disputed amount, **plus** any chargeback fees we incur (typically $20–$30 per chargeback) and reasonable investigation/legal costs;
- treat outstanding balances as a debt and refer them to collection;
- **terminate** your Account under ToS Section 11.2.

5.3 We will provide evidence to the card network or PayPal in support of legitimate charges, including (a) signed Terms of Service, (b) acknowledgment logs from `05e`, (c) usage records, (d) IP and device fingerprints, and (e) communication logs.

5.4 A chargeback is not a substitute for requesting a refund under this policy.

## 6. EU / UK / EEA Consumer Right of Withdrawal

6.1 If you are a **consumer** resident in the EU, UK, EEA, or another jurisdiction granting an equivalent statutory withdrawal right, you have the right to withdraw from the subscription contract within **14 calendar days** of the date you accepted the Terms and paid, **without giving any reason**.

6.2 **How to exercise.** Send an unambiguous statement of withdrawal (email or letter) to `billing@gofunnelai.com` within the 14-day window. You may use the model withdrawal form in Section 6.6.

6.3 **Effects of withdrawal.** We will reimburse all payments received from you for the withdrawn subscription, using the same payment method you used, **within 14 days** of receiving your withdrawal notice.

6.4 **Provision started during the withdrawal period.** By starting to use the Service during the 14-day withdrawal window, you expressly request that performance begin before the period ends and acknowledge that, under EU Consumer Rights Directive Article 16(m) and equivalents, the right to withdraw may be lost once the digital service has been fully supplied with your prior express consent and acknowledgment that you lose the right. **Where you have used the Service during the withdrawal period, we will deduct an amount proportionate to the services already provided.** You will be asked to acknowledge this at checkout.

6.5 **Does not affect statutory rights.** This withdrawal right is in addition to, and does not affect, your other statutory consumer rights (including the right to a conformant digital service under EU Directive 2019/770).

## 7. Other Jurisdictions

- **California (Auto-Renewal Law)** and similar state laws: clear and conspicuous disclosure of auto-renewal terms is given at signup; you can cancel online from your Account at any time.
- **Brazil (CDC)**: 7-day right of regret for distance contracts (Art. 49 CDC) applies in addition; reach out to `billing@gofunnelai.com`.
- **Australia (ACL)**, **New Zealand (CGA)**, **Canada (provincial consumer protection)**, **India (Consumer Protection Act 2019)**, **South Africa (CPA)**: mandatory consumer protections apply on top of this policy.

Where mandatory local law gives you greater rights than this policy, those rights prevail.
