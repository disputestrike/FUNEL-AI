## 11. Prohibited Claims

Banned: "guaranteed regrowth," "100% permanent" (donor is permanent, but transplanted into recipient area requires honest expectation), "no side effects," "miracle hair growth," "FDA approved for [off-label use]," before/after without HIPAA auth, testimonials without auth or paid undisclosed (FTC), comparing compounded to branded as "the same."

## 12. Compliance Rules

**HIPAA Privacy / Security / Marketing Rules (45 CFR §164):** PHI marketing requires auth. **HHS Online Tracking Tech (2023, 2024):** Pixel on patient pages = HIPAA risk. **FDA approval:** finasteride 1mg / 5mg approved for men (not women); minoxidil topical 2%/5%; compounded topical finasteride is off-label / 503A-regulated. **Pregnant women & finasteride exposure warning** required. **State Medical Board:** licensure; cosmetic surgery scope. **ABHRS Diplomate** is appropriate cert claim. **TCPA / CAN-SPAM / FTC §5.** **FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR §255):** influencer #ad. **FTC Health Products Compliance Guidance (2022):** substantiate. **State Pharmacy Board** for 503A compounding. **OR accreditation** if office-based surgical (AAAHC / Joint Commission). **ADA/WCAG 2.1 AA.**
