# GoFunnelAI KB Pack — Residential Cleaning Services (US-EN)

> Pack ID: `cleaning-us-en` | Vertical: Residential house cleaning (recurring + one-time + deep + move-out) | Geo: US | Language: EN

## 1. Market Overview

US residential cleaning is $40B in 2025 (residential portion of $80B total industry), growing 6–8% YoY [industry estimate based on ISSA + IBISWorld 2025]. Approximately 16M US households use a paid cleaning service at least once a year; ~9M maintain recurring (biweekly is dominant cadence). Average ticket: $120–$190 standard biweekly clean (2–3 BR / 2 BA); $250–$450 first/deep clean; $380–$680 move-out clean; $480–$1,200 post-construction clean. Annual recurring contract value: $3,000–$5,200 per household.

Sales cycle: same-day to 5 days. Gross margin: 32–48% (W-2 model) or 50–62% (sub/1099 — but legal risk). LTV: $4,800 over 2.4 years. Acceptable CAC: $80–$220. Acceptable CPL: $14–$42 at 22–38% close rate.

Buying triggers: (1) baby on the way; (2) new home purchase / move-in or move-out; (3) hosting holiday; (4) post-construction; (5) elder-care transition; (6) returning to office (no time); (7) Airbnb host needing turnover; (8) divorce / move; (9) allergy / asthma flare; (10) seasonal deep clean (spring + pre-holiday).

## 2. Buyer Personas

**Persona 1: Dual-Income Time-Starved Family (44%).** 30–50, kids 0–14, $130k–$280k HHI, suburban. JTBD: "Take cleaning off our plate so we can be with kids on weekend." Verbatim: "I just want it to look like a hotel when I walk in." Fear: stranger in the house, theft, broken things. Winning: same cleaner each visit, background-checked, insured.

**Persona 2: Move-Out / Move-In One-Time (22%).** Renter or homebuyer. JTBD: "Get deposit back" or "start clean in new home." Winning: walkthrough guarantee, landlord-approved checklist.

**Persona 3: Senior / Empty Nester Lifecycler (14%).** 60–80, kids gone, can no longer maintain. JTBD: "Reliable monthly clean." Fear: stranger, schedule reliability.

**Persona 4: Holiday Host (12%).** 30–60, hosting Thanksgiving / Christmas / dinner party. JTBD: "Spotless within 72 hours."

**Persona 5: Airbnb / STR Host (8%).** 30–55, side-hustle owner. JTBD: "Reliable turnover within checkout window."

- **O3 — "What if they break something?"** Rebuttal: "$1M liability + written claim process."
- **O4 — "Cancel anytime?"** Rebuttal: "Yes, no penalty."

## 12. Compliance Rules

**TCPA / CAN-SPAM / FTC §5** standard. **EPA Safer Choice** program for chemical claims. **OSHA Hazard Communication Standard (29 CFR §1910.1200)** — SDS available for every product used. **State wage-hour:** if employees are 1099 misclassified, AG action risk (CA AB-5, MA wage law). **Background-check disclosure** under FCRA (15 USC §1681) — written notice + consent required. **State home-service contractor acts** vary. **Workers' Comp** required by state in nearly all jurisdictions. **ADA/WCAG 2.1 AA.**
