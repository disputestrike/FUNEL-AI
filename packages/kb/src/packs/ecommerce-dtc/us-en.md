# GoFunnelAI KB Pack — DTC E-commerce (US-EN)

> Pack ID: `ecommerce-dtc-us-en` | Vertical: Direct-to-consumer e-commerce (apparel, home, beauty, accessories, gifting) — non-supplement, non-health | Geo: US | Language: EN

## 1. Market Overview

US DTC e-commerce GMV is $258B in 2025, growing 11–14% YoY [eMarketer, Shopify Plus 2025]. Approximately 2.3M active US DTC brands on Shopify, with ~120,000 doing >$1M/yr. Average AOV by category: apparel $68, home goods $94, beauty $52, accessories $46, gifting $74. Repeat-purchase rate target: 28–42%. LTV:CAC target: 3:1. Acceptable CAC: $24–$58 prospect, $8–$18 retargeting. Acceptable CPL/CPA: $14–$48 cold acquisition.

Sales cycle: 1 day to 14 days (most <72 hours from first ad click to first purchase). Mobile checkout: 78% of traffic, 64% of revenue. Email + SMS drive 28–38% of revenue for mature brands.

Buying triggers: (1) creator/UGC content discovery; (2) gifting events (Mother's Day, Father's Day, Valentine's, Christmas); (3) seasonal apparel changes; (4) BFCM (Black Friday/Cyber Monday); (5) influencer endorsement; (6) Prime Day comp pricing; (7) sale/restock; (8) reviews surfaced via Google Shopping.

## 2. Buyer Personas

**Persona 1: Mobile-First Impulse Buyer (38%).** 24–42, scrolls Reels/TikTok, $40k–$120k HHI. JTBD: "Looks cool, I want it." Verbatim: "Just buy it." Winning: video creative + 1-click checkout + AB-tested PDP.

**Persona 2: Considered-Purchase Researcher (22%).** 28–55, reads reviews + comparison. JTBD: "Make sure it's worth it." Winning: 100+ verified reviews, comparison page, video PDP.

**Persona 3: Gift-Giver (16%).** 30–55, occasional. JTBD: "Get a great gift fast." Winning: gift-finder quiz, gift wrap, dated delivery.

**Persona 4: Loyal Repeat (14%).** Past buyer. JTBD: "Get more, exclusive drops." Winning: VIP program + early access + texts.

**Persona 5: Coupon Hunter (10%).** Won't buy without promo. JTBD: "Find the discount." Winning: exit-intent code, loyalty points.

## 11. Prohibited Claims

Per FTC Section 5, FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR 255), FTC "Made in USA" Labeling Rule (16 CFR Part 323), CA Prop 65, state UDAP, ROSCA (negative-option), CARS Rule (N/A): no fake reviews, no influencer endorsements without #ad, no "Made in USA" if not, no fake scarcity, no fake reference price (markdown from never-sold price), no fake countdown, no missing material terms on subscription, must clearly disclose recurring charges and cancellation.

**Hard-block exact strings:**
- `"100% Made in USA"` (without compliance)
- `"only 3 left forever"`
- `"price drop ends midnight"` (unless real)
- `"was $299 now $99"` (without history)
- `"limited edition forever"`
- `"guaranteed lowest price"`
- `"clinically proven"` (non-supplement claims)
- `"FDA approved"`
- `"cures"`
- `"prevents disease"`
- `"miracle product"`
- `"better than [competitor]"` (without comparable test)
- `"act before midnight"`
- `"countdown timer fake"`
- `"free trial then auto charge"` (without ROSCA)
- `"#1 in the world"`
- `"endorsed by celebrities"` (without disclosure)
- `"reviews verified by us"` (if fake)
- `"clinical results"` (without data)
- `"organic"` (without USDA cert)

## 12. Compliance Rules

**FTC Section 5 + Endorsement Guides (16 CFR 255):** #ad on paid; verified reviews only; UGC release. **FTC Made in USA Labeling Rule (16 CFR 323).** **ROSCA (15 USC 8403)** for negative-option subscriptions: clear pre-purchase disclosure, simple cancellation. **Click-to-Cancel Rule (FTC 2024).** **CAN-SPAM + TCPA** (10DLC + STOP/HELP). **State sales tax (Wayfair nexus thresholds).** **CCPA / CPRA (CA) + CO/CT/VA/UT** data privacy. **CA Prop 65** warnings for applicable products. **PCI DSS** card data. **GDPR** if EU traffic. **ADA/WCAG 2.1 AA.** **FTC Consumer Review Fairness Act:** no non-disparagement clauses.

## 22. Pitfalls
