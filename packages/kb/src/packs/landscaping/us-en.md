## 1. Market Overview

US residential landscaping services market is $69B in 2025, growing 4–6% YoY [industry estimate based on NALP + IBISWorld 2025]. Approximately 22M US households use a paid lawn/landscape service. Average ticket: $48–$120 per recurring mow visit; $1,200–$4,800 seasonal cleanup; $3,500–$28,000 design-install (planting beds, sod, mulch); $8,000–$120,000 hardscape (patio, retaining wall, outdoor kitchen); $1,800–$8,500 irrigation install; $480–$3,200 tree pruning per tree; $4,500–$15,000 tree removal large.

Annual recurring revenue per recurring lawn customer: $2,400–$5,200. Gross margin: 38–55% on mow/maintain; 24–35% on installs; 22–32% on hardscape. Sales cycle: same-day to 9 days for service, 14–45 days for design/build. LTV: $9,800 over 4 years recurring. Acceptable CAC: $80–$280 (recurring), $400–$1,200 (install). Acceptable CPL: $18–$58 recurring, $48–$140 install.

Buying triggers: (1) HOA violation; (2) hosting backyard event; (3) new home / first lawn; (4) curb-appeal pre-sale; (5) damaged tree post-storm; (6) drought-tolerant conversion (CA, AZ, NV — rebates); (7) outdoor living movement (patio, kitchen, fire-pit); (8) irrigation broken or wasting water; (9) spring start-up / fall cleanup; (10) pest/weed taking over.

## 12. Compliance Rules

**TCPA / CAN-SPAM / FTC §5** standard. **FIFRA (7 USC §136) + EPA pesticide registration.** **State pesticide applicator license** required (e.g., CA DPR; FL FDACS; TX TDA; NY DEC). Display license # on every ad. **Gas blower bans:** CA AB-1346 (2024); DC; multiple CA cities; check city/county. **ISA Certified Arborist** recommended for tree work; some jurisdictions require Tree Service License (NJ, MD). **Stormwater / Erosion control:** state permits for >1 acre disturbance. **State contractor licensing for hardscape:** CA C-27 / C-29; AZ ROC; FL CSC; TX TDLR. **Water-rebate programs:** SoCal Met SoCalWater$mart; Las Vegas SNWA Water Smart; AZ APS turf removal. **ADA/WCAG 2.1 AA.**
