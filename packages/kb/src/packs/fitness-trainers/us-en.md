## 12. Compliance Rules

**FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR §255)** — testimonials with typical-results disclosure. **FTC Health Claims (2022 guidance)** — substantiation. **FTC ROSCA + Click-to-Cancel** for memberships. **State scope-of-practice** for nutrition: CA Business & Professions Code §2585+, FL §468.501+, OH ORC §4759 — many states restrict nutrition counseling to licensed dietitians; PTs can give general nutrition info but not medical nutrition therapy. **State scope-of-practice** for "treatment" / "rehab" — PT (physical therapy) is licensed and PTs (personal trainers) cannot claim to do physical therapy. **TCPA / CAN-SPAM.** **Liability waivers** — state-specific enforceability. **PCI-DSS** for membership billing. **ADA accommodation** for clients. **HIPAA does NOT apply to PTs** unless covered entity. **State health-club consumer protection laws** (NY GBL §394-d; CA Civil §1812.80+) — contract length caps + cancellation rights. **ADA / WCAG 2.1 AA.**
